### **Pizza Sales Analysis | SQL + Power BI**



📌 Project Overview



This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover business insights related to revenue performance, product demand and customer ordering patterns. The analysis is performed using SQL for data querying and KPI calculation and Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization. The goal of this project is to demonstrate data analysis, SQL querying and dashboard storytelling skills.



#### **Tools \& Technologies**



* SQL (Data extraction \& KPI calculations)
* Power BI (Data visualization \& dashboard)
* DAX (Measures \& calculations)



#### **Dashboard Structure**



##### Page 1 – Product Performance Overview



This page provides a summary of key business metrics and product-level performance.



**Key KPIs**



* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Pizzas Sold
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Average Pizzas Per Order



**Analysis**



* Revenue by Pizza Category
* Orders by Pizza Size
* Top 5 Best Selling Pizzas
* Bottom 5 Least Selling Pizzas



This section helps identify which pizza categories and sizes contribute most to sales.



##### Page 2 – Time-Based Sales Analysis



This page focuses on understanding when customers place orders.



**Analysis**



* Revenue by Month
* Orders by Month
* Orders by Day of Week
* Orders by Hour of Day



These insights help identify peak ordering periods and seasonal trends.



#### **Key Insights**



* Classic Pizza Category contributed maximum revenue followed by Supreme
* Large and medium pizza sizes account for a major share of orders
* Classic Deluxe and Barbeque chicken are top sellers 
* Mediterranean and Brie care pizza are least ordered
* July month had most number of orders followed by May
* Most of the orders are on weekends
* Peak order activity occurs at 12 pm and from 4pm - 8pm
